✦ Features

Two modes. One purpose.

Whether you're tracking a full year or a custom challenge, LockIn adapts to your timeline.

Setup Mode

Set your goal title, choose between Year or Custom countdown, and activate the wallpaper in one tap. A live preview shows exactly how your lock screen will look.

  • Goal title up to 24 characters
  • Full Year (365/366 days) or Custom duration
  • Real-time lock screen preview

Progress Mode

Like a GitHub contribution graph, each day's dot color reflects your task completion ratio. Complete everything for a bright dot, skip for a dim one.

  • Add custom daily tasks
  • Toggle completion — streaks visible instantly
  • Auto-resets at midnight

✦ How It Works

Set it once. See progress daily.

LockIn requires no daily effort. Configure it once and your lock screen becomes your accountability dashboard.

1

Set Your Goal

Name your mission — "JEE 2026", "75 Hard", anything up to 24 characters. Short titles fit best on the lock screen.

2

Choose Countdown Mode

Full Year (365/366 days) for annual goals, or Custom Days for any duration — 30, 90, 180 days, whatever fits.

3

Activate Live Wallpaper

One tap sets your lock screen as a live progress dashboard. Current day pulses. Past days glow. Future days wait.

4

Enable Progress Mode

Add daily tasks. Check them off. Each day's dot color reflects your completion ratio — building streaks you can see.

5

Customize Colors

Pick your background, accent, completed, and remaining colors. Five presets for each. Make it yours.

6

Add Home Screen Widget

Your progress visible without even unlocking your phone. Works on Android home screen and iOS widgets.
